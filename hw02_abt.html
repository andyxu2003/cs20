About Me

Born and raised in a small town, I always had a passion for animals. As a child, I volunteered at the local animal shelter and spent all of my free time caring for stray animals. As I grew up, I pursued my passion by studying veterinary science in college. However, after completing my studies, I realized that my true calling was not in veterinary medicine, but in providing care and attention to animals in their own homes as a pet sitter. I found joy in getting to know each pet on a personal level and providing them with love and comfort while their owners were away With years of experience and a deep love for animals, I am one of the most trusted and reliable pet sitters in the greater Boston area.

I have a 5-year old lab named Cooper (pictured to the right), and an 8-year old Siamese cat Nala. Both of them are very comfortable around other animals. Feel free to contact me with any questions, and I can't wait to love your loved ones!
